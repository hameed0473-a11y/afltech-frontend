Refund Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026 | Effective: June 27, 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with Contributions Manager. If you experience an issue with your purchase, please contact us within the eligible period and we will make it right.

1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to purchases made for the Lite (₹240/year) and Pro (₹2,832/year) plans of Contributions Manager, operated by AFTechs.

2. Refund Eligibility

✅ Eligible for Refund

  • Within 7 days of purchase — if you are not satisfied with the App for any reason
  • Payment processed but plan not activated — if you were charged but your account was not upgraded
  • Duplicate payment — if you were charged twice for the same plan
  • Technical failure — if a documented technical issue prevented you from using the paid features

❌ Not Eligible for Refund

  • Requests made after 7 days from the purchase date
  • If you have used the paid features extensively during the period
  • Change of mind after 7 days
  • Failure to cancel before using the plan for more than 30 days
  • Issues caused by your device, internet connection, or third-party services
  • Purchases made through Google Play — these are subject to Google Play's own refund policy

4. Google Play Purchases

If you purchased through Google Play, refunds are subject to Google Play's refund policy. You can request a refund directly through the Play Store within 48 hours of purchase. For requests after 48 hours, contact us at admin@aftechs.in.

5. Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial refunds for unused subscription time. If your refund is approved, the full amount paid will be refunded and your account will be downgraded to the Basic plan.

6. Subscription Expiry

We do not offer refunds for expired subscriptions. Please ensure you renew your subscription before it expires to avoid losing paid features. You will receive reminder notifications before expiry.
