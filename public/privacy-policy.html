2.2 App Usage Data (Basic & Lite Users)

All your contribution records, targets, goals, contributors, and pledges are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload or access this data.

2.3 App Usage Data (Pro Users)

Pro users' contribution records, targets, goals, contributors, and pledges are stored on our secure cloud servers (Supabase) for backup and sync purposes.

2.4 Payment Data

Payment processing is handled by Razorpay and/or Google Play Billing. We do not store your card or bank details. We only receive payment confirmation (success/failure) and the transaction ID.

2.5 Advertising Data (Basic Plan Only)

Basic plan users see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Lite and Pro users do not see ads.

2.6 Analytics Data

We collect basic registration statistics (number of signups, country, plan type) for internal analytics. No personally identifiable usage patterns are tracked.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To authenticate you securely via password and MPIN
  • To verify your subscription status (Lite/Pro)
  • To sync and backup data for Pro users
  • To prevent fraud and unauthorized access (device ID binding)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To send service-critical notifications (subscription expiry, security alerts)

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or selling to third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Basic/Lite users: Account tracking data retained for 3 years from last activity, then deleted
  • Pro users: Cloud data retained while subscription is active + 1 year after expiry
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law
  • You may request deletion at any time (see Section 7)

6. Data Security

  • Passwords are stored using bcrypt hashing (one-way encryption)
  • MPINs are stored using bcrypt hashing
  • All server communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Device ID binding prevents unauthorized use of Pro/Lite subscriptions
  • Pro cloud data is stored in Supabase with row-level security

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data (Pro users — contact support)
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, email admin@aftechs.in with subject "Data Request — [your mobile number]". We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notification. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
